Privacy Policy for CodeVerse

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CodeVerse, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (when you sign up or sign in using email, Google, or GitHub)
  • Usage Data: AI conversations, token usage, articles read, progress tracking, bookmarks
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system (collected automatically for app functionality)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI mentor and learning features
  • Manage your account and track your learning progress
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications (if you opt in)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • OpenAI: For AI mentor responses and chat functionality
  • Google: For Google Sign-In authentication
  • GitHub: For GitHub Sign-In authentication
  • Hosting Provider: For backend services and secure data storage

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our backend servers. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection
  • Update or correct your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

5.1. Account Deletion and Data Removal

How to Request Account Deletion:

To request deletion of your CodeVerse account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@learn-made.in with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your account email address in the email body
  3. Confirm your identity by providing any additional information we may request for verification
  4. Wait for confirmation - We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted:

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account information (email address, name, profile data)
  • All AI conversation history
  • Learning progress and tracking data
  • Bookmarks and saved articles
  • Token usage records
  • Authentication tokens and session data

Data Retention Period:

After you submit a deletion request, your account and data will be deleted within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a longer period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or legal compliance).

Data Shared with Third Parties:

Please note that data previously shared with third-party services (such as OpenAI for AI mentor responses) may have been processed by those services according to their own privacy policies. We will delete data from our systems, but we cannot guarantee deletion from third-party systems beyond our control.

For Parents/Guardians:

If you are a parent or guardian requesting deletion of a child's account (under 13), please include the child's account email and your relationship to the child in your request. We will verify your identity before processing the deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

CodeVerse is designed for users aged 9 and above. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with applicable laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union.

For users under 13: If you are under 13 years of age, please have your parent or guardian review this privacy policy and help you create an account. We may require parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13.

For parents and guardians: If you believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately. You have the right to review, request deletion of, or refuse further collection of your child's personal information.

Data collected from children: When children use CodeVerse, we collect the same types of information as described in Section 1 (account information, usage data, device information). This data is used solely to provide educational services and improve the learning experience.

Third-party services: We share children's data with third-party services (OpenAI for AI mentor) only as necessary to provide the service. We ensure these services comply with applicable children's privacy laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
